Our Story

Isoko ry'Ubumenyi, which means "Source of Knowledge" in Kinyarwanda, was founded in 2019 with a simple yet powerful vision: to make quality education accessible to every Rwandan student, regardless of their location or background.

We recognized that many talented students across Rwanda lacked access to quality educational resources and expert instructors. By leveraging technology and partnering with the Rwanda Education Board (REB), we've created a platform that brings world-class education directly to students' homes.

Today, we serve over 10,000 students across all provinces, offering courses aligned with the national curriculum and beyond. Our journey has just begun, and we're committed to continuous innovation in education.
